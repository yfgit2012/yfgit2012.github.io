## Boris Cherny: Claude Code

**Summary**  
This article discusses how Boris Cherny disrupted the software engineering industry through his non-traditional background and innovative thinking. Transitioning from economics, he built full-stack capabilities through early entrepreneurship, later drove user-centric product design at Meta, and joined Anthropic to develop Claude Code, revolutionizing AI programming tools. His career highlights the structural transformation of software engineering by AI technology, emphasizing the need for engineers to shift from code producers to collaborative designers, while advocating for a user-focused approach with attention to AI safety and ethics.

**Key Points**  
- **Non-traditional Background and Innovative Thinking**  
  Boris, not a computer science graduate, dropped out to start a business but succeeded at Meta and Anthropic with his full-stack capabilities (coding, product design, user research).  
- **User-Centric Product Design**  
  At Meta, he identified latent user needs by observing behaviors (e.g., cafeteria testing), leading to the creation of Facebook Marketplace and Dating features, stressing the importance of uncovering users' unmet intentions.  
- **Transition from Meta to Anthropic**  
  He chose Anthropic over higher salaries or positions due to his focus on AI safety and ethics. Anthropic's "safety-first" culture aligned with his values, driving the development of Claude Code.  
- **Revolutionary Design of Claude Code**  
  This tool wasn’t aimed at solving current AI coding limitations but was designed for future model capabilities (e.g., Sonnet, Opus 4), enabling a leap from auxiliary tools to primary production methods, with 80%-90% of internal code generated by it.  
- **New Model of Human-AI Collaboration**  
  Engineers and AI divide roles: AI handles repetitive coding, while humans focus on core logic and quality control, boosting overall productivity. Non-technical roles (e.g., sales, analysts) can also participate in software development via natural language instructions.  
- **Structural Impact of AI on Software Engineering**  
  While AI lowers coding barriers, software engineering thresholds remain high. Engineers must master business understanding, architecture design, and risk prediction, rather than relying solely on AI-generated code.  
- **Reflections on AI Ethics and the Future**  
  Boris emphasizes the need to address long-term risks (e.g., biosecurity, societal systemic damage) and encourages engineers to actively embrace AI tools rather than resist change, to enhance their value.

- **非传统背景与创新思维**  
  鲍里斯并非计算机科班出身，曾辍学创业，但凭借全栈能力（写代码、产品设计、用户研究）在Meta和Anthropic取得成功。
