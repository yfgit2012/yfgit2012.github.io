3. **Engineering Challenges and Commercial Viability:**  
   - **Nine Steps Rule (Nine-Step Rule):** Current models face a reliability gap between "sawtooth" intelligence (limited, fragmented capabilities) and fully autonomous agents. The "Iron Man Armor" (short-term) is the only feasible commercial path, while "Iron Man Robot" (long-term) represents a vision for fully autonomous AI.

4. **Infrastructure Reforms for AI Agents:**

   - **MCP Protocol:** Standardizes tool interfaces for AI agents, allowing seamless interaction with SaaS platforms via JSON Schema. This reduces integration entropy and enables real-time state queries for task planning.
   - **Context Builders (e.g., Gitingest):** Preprocess real-world data (e.g., GitHub repositories) into compact, memory-efficient formats, acting as "memory controllers" for LLMs.

   - **Intent as Core Value:** Humans retain unique value through intent generation and high-entropy thinking (e.g., creativity, non-rational ideas). AI excels at execution but lacks the capacity to define problems or express emotional intent.

   - **AI's Entropy Convergence:** AI-generated data tends to average out training distributions, leading to "model collapse" where systems become narrow, safe, and uncreative. Human input (high-entropy, unpredictable ideas) is essential to maintain diversity and prevent stagnation.

   - **Symbiosis Over Replacement:** The future lies in human-AI symbiosis, where humans define intent and creativity, while AI handles execution. This balance ensures both systems thrive without one overshadowing the other.
