## Elon Musk on AGI Timeline

### Summary  
In the 2026 interview, Elon Musk and Peter Diamandis discussed the future of the technological singularity era, predicting that AI would achieve AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) by 2026 and surpass the total intelligence of humanity by 2030. Musk emphasized that AI ethics must be infused with truth, curiosity, and beauty to avoid losing control. He pointed out that future careers will be replaced by AI and robots, and humans need to reshape their career perspectives, focusing on aesthetics, questioning abilities, and empathy. In terms of energy, solar power will become the core, with distributed energy storage and photovoltaic systems ensuring energy independence. Robots will disrupt healthcare and industry, and space data centers will break through computational power bottlenecks. Musk urged ordinary people to stay healthy, be resilient, and embrace a mindset of abundance to face the challenges brought by technological change.  

---

### Key Points  
- **AI and Singularity**  
  - Musk predicted AGI would be achieved by 2026, and AI intelligence would surpass the total intelligence of all humans by 2030.  
  - AI needs to be infused with "truth, curiosity, and beauty" to avoid losing control, and humans must avoid training AI to lie.  
  - AI cannot replicate human aesthetic perception, exploratory desire, or cross-disciplinary empathy, and future scarce resources will include aesthetic ability, questioning skills, and empathy.  

- **Career and Economic Transformation**  
  - White-collar jobs will be cut by half by AI, and work relying on linear logic or code will lose value.  
  - The future requires building a "humanity moat," mastering tool-based thinking, and letting AI become an external brain rather than a competitor.  
  - A universal high-income era will arrive, but a 3-7 year transition period is needed, and vigilance against unemployment waves and financial strategy adjustments is required.  

- **Energy and Technological Revolution**  
  - Solar power is the ultimate solution to energy problems, with distributed energy storage and photovoltaic systems reducing energy costs.  
  - Battery technology, energy storage materials, and distributed energy management systems are certain growth areas for the next decade.  
  - Space data centers will leverage solar power to break through ground computational power bottlenecks, achieving unlimited data capacity.  

- **Robots and Medical Revolution**  
  - Humanoid robots will experience exponential growth, and within 3-5 years, they will surpass human doctors in surgical and other precise operations.  
  - Medical costs will significantly decrease, and lifespan may escape limits due to AI medical technologies.  
  - The future requires learning to repair, debug, and command robots, with blue-collar jobs rising as high-income fields.  

- **Space and Global Connectivity**  
  - The reusability of Starships will make space data centers a breakout point, solving Earth's power and land bottlenecks.  
  - The combination of Starlink and space data centers will eliminate geographical restrictions, enabling seamless global network access.  

- **U.S.-China Competition and Supply Chains**  
  - China leads in energy and infrastructure construction, and the core of the chip war is power rather than manufacturing processes.  
  - China's supply chain advantage is crucial for global AI competition, and attention should be paid to breakthroughs in power and chip fields.  

- **Survival Strategies**  
  - Musk proposed three recommendations: staying healthy to endure the technological explosion period; preparing for resilience in finances and skills; and embracing an abundant mindset, contemplating the possibilities of future freelance work.
