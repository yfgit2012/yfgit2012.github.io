## Jensen Huang interview at Joe Rogan Experience 

**Article Summary**  
In a deep interview with Joe Rogan, Huang Renxun discusses from multiple dimensions including energy strategy, AI development, corporate history, and personal growth. He emphasizes that the essence of the AI competition is an energy competition, calling on the United States to develop energy to maintain technological hegemony; through NVIDIA's early challenges (such as abandoning the quadrilateral technology route), he demonstrates strategic courage; and explains AI capabilities via the "universal function approximator," urging humans to master AI with a defensive mindset. Meanwhile, he shares his transformation from an immigrant teenager to a tech giant, revealing his success stems from reverence for failure, insight into the essence of technology, and commitment to long-termism.

---

**Key Points**  
- **Connection Between Energy and AI**  
  Huang Renxun believes AI development relies on computing power and energy. The U.S. needs to prioritize clean energy development to support the technological revolution, or else it will be surpassed by other countries.  

- **AI Safety and Defensive Mindset**  
  He proposes that AI's "universal function approximator" characteristic means it can simulate complex patterns, but humans must approach potential risks with a defensive perspective rather than solely pursuing technological breakthroughs.  

- **NVIDIA's Early Turning Point**  
  - In 1993, Huang Renxun abandoned the quadrilateral technology route, admitted his mistake, and shifted to the CUDA platform, driving GPUs from graphics processing to general computing.  
  - In 2012, through CUDA support for the AlexNet model, he laid the foundation for deep learning and gifted the DGX-1 supercomputer to OpenAI, accelerating AI development.  

- **Huang Renxun's Personal Philosophy**  
  - **Fear-Driven Success**: He admits success stems from fear of failure, maintaining a "Suffering Mindset," believing pain and setbacks are essential for growth.  
  - **Technological Accessibility**: AI lowers the technical threshold, allowing ordinary people to collaborate with AI via natural language rather than opposing the technological wave.  

- **Vision for the Future**  
  Huang Renxun predicts AI capabilities will grow exponentially, urging humans to remain vigilant and learn to address the opportunities and challenges brought by technological change.
